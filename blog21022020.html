Weekly Report 21 February 2020

Published: 22/02/2020

Following another positive week, the reaction from investors to world news has been mixed. The economic effect of SARS-CoV-2 has began to slow down technology and manufacturing sectors, who rely heavily on the Chinese market. It’s tough to tell how much of an impact this will have on the global economy, as there haven’t been any improvements on containing the spread of the virus. While the current effects of the outbreak can’t cause an economic crash, if the current trends continue, we could be looking at a global recession within the next few months. However, this isn’t a case to start panicking or closing your positions, recessions are a natural part of the economic cycle and offer a great buying opportunity for savvy investors.
